## Code of Conduct

Fydemy is a community for learners, mentors, contributors, and speakers.  
We are committed to providing a **welcoming, safe, and inclusive** environment for everyone.

By participating in this project (issues, pull requests, discussions, events, and the Discord community), you agree to uphold this Code of Conduct.

---

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

- **Being respectful and empathetic** toward other people.
- **Offering constructive feedback** and accepting it gracefully.
- **Focusing on what is best for learners and the community.**
- **Using inclusive language** and being mindful of different backgrounds and experiences.

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Disrespectful or derogatory comments, trolling, or insults.
- Publishing others' private information without permission.
- Any other conduct that could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional/learning setting.

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within:

- This repository and its issue/PR trackers.
- Official Fydemy communication channels (e.g. Discord).
- Community events, online or in person, that are officially associated with Fydemy.

---

## Reporting

If you experience or witness behavior that violates this Code of Conduct:

- Reach out via the Fydemy Discord (e.g. to moderators or admins), or  
- Use any official contact channel listed on `https://fydemy.com`.

All reports will be reviewed and handled confidentially to the extent possible.

---

## Enforcement

Project maintainers and community moderators may take any action they deem appropriate, including:

- Asking a participant to stop unacceptable behavior.
- Temporarily or permanently removing access to community spaces.
- Escalation to platform support if necessary (e.g. GitHub, Discord).

By engaging with this project and the Fydemy community, you agree to abide by this Code of Conduct.
