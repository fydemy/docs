This is a place where our open-source projects are documented also with the learning paths. Steps that you have to do:

1. Join our community
2. Choose role `student`, `mentor`, `contributor`, `speaker`
3. Introduce yourself at _#welcome_ channel
4. Done!

## Roles explained

1. **Student/others**: Ask anything at the topics category and learn from our learning path here or participate in weekly session every `Wednesday, 20.00 WIB` and `Friday, 20.00 WIB` or join the study hall anytime on Discord at _#grinding-hall_ channel.
2. **Mentor**: Open for discussion and willing to share your thoughts on the community.
3. **Contributor**: Open for our Github projects contribution, shares ideas, feedback, and progress.
4. **Speaker**: Willing to share and get personal branding through our social media. Just info us at _#small-talk channel_.
